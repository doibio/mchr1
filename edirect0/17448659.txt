Design and synthesis of novel hydantoin-containing melanin-concentrating hormone 
receptor antagonists.

Balavoine F(1), Malabre P, Alleaume T, Rey A, Cherfils V, Jeanneton O, 
Seigneurin-Venin S, Revah F.

We report here new chemical series acting as antagonists of 
melanin-concentrating hormone receptor 1 (MCHR-1). Synthesis and 
structure-activity relationships are described leading to the identification of 
compounds with optimized in vitro pharmacological and in vitro ADME profiles. In 
vivo activity has been demonstrated in animal models of food intake and 
depression.
